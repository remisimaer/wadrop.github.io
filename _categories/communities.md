Find event listings (Meetups, Camps), discussion channels (Slack, forums), and local user groups to engage with the Japanese Drupal community.

* * *

### WaDrop.org Discussions Forum

The official discussions forum for WaDrop.org. This is the place to ask questions, share ideas, propose improvements to the repository, and engage with the community about Drupal in Japan. All contributions are welcome!

[Join WaDrop.org Discussions](https://github.com/remisimaer/wadrop.github.io/discussions)

* * *

### Connpass (Events & Meetups)

The go-to platform for tech events in Japan. This is the central hub for finding and registering for local "Drupal Meetups" (Tokyo, Kansai, Sapporo) and "Drupal Cafes." Check this site regularly to ensure you don't miss any in-person or online gatherings.

[Visit Connpass](https://connpass.com/)

* * *

### Drupal Japan Slack

The historic Slack workspace entirely dedicated to the local community. This is the heart of daily discussions in Japanese, ranging from technical support and Q&A to the logistical organization of local events.

[Join Drupal Japan Slack](https://drupal-japan.slack.com/)

* * *

### Official Drupal Slack (#japan channel)

The primary bridge between Japan and the global community. Joining this channel on the official Drupal Slack allows you to stay connected with global initiatives while interacting with Japanese developers present on the international stage.

[Join Official Drupal Slack](https://drupal.slack.com/join/shared_invite/zt-3k9bnj6zl-49Ic3hg0z_iOt0WQVCVKXg#/shared-invite/email)

* * *

### Drupal.org Japan Group

The official presence of the Japanese community on the Drupal.org website. While fast-paced discussions happen on Slack, this is where major initiatives, institutional information, and long-term documentation are housed.

[Visit Japan Group](https://groups.drupal.org/japan)
