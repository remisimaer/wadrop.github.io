Japan has a dynamic ecosystem of companies specializing in the development and maintenance of Drupal-based solutions. These companies play a key role in the digital transformation of Japanese organizations, ranging from public administrations to large private enterprises, including educational institutions and media.

This directory presents profiles of companies with expertise in Drupal services, technical positioning, and contributions to the Drupal community.

Companies are displayed in alphabetical order.

Missing a company? The website is open-source, you can contribute [here](https://github.com/remisimaer/wadrop.github.io).

* * *

### ANNAI

Based in Tokyo and founded in 2007, ANNAI is a company specializing in web system development with Drupal. They focus on comprehensive support from development to maintenance, with recognized expertise in Agile development and complex system integration. ANNAI works with regional governments, universities, and major enterprises.

[Learn More](/companies/annai)

* * *

### Digital Circus

Founded in 1999 in Tokyo with approximately 44 employees, Digital Circus is the first Japanese company certified as an official Drupal Association partner. They offer comprehensive Drupal services including development, maintenance, consulting, and training. Notable projects include Japan Airlines, mainichi.jp (one of Japan's largest news sites), and various e-commerce platforms.

[Learn More](/companies/digital-circus)

* * *

### Genero Inc.

Based in Tokyo, Genero Inc. specializes in digital transformation (DX) and Drupal-based system development. They stand out with their multilingual approach and ability to support enterprise digital transformation through custom Drupal solutions. Their bilingual team (English and Japanese) provides comprehensive support from consulting to cloud operations.

[Learn More](/companies/genero)

* * *

### MetaInfo

Founded in 2018 and based in Tokyo, MetaInfo focuses on creating sustainable and scalable systems with Drupal. They emphasize the use of simple, combinable, and extensible functional blocks to meet client needs. MetaInfo has strong involvement in the open-source community and specializes in educational portals, enterprise solutions, and e-commerce platforms.

[Learn More](/companies/metainfo)

* * *

### Mochiya

Based in Tokyo, Mochiya offers deep technical expertise in designing, developing, and operating Drupal-based systems. They specialize in building complex enterprise portals and migrating existing systems to Drupal. A notable achievement includes consolidating 1,500 internal NEC sites into a single Drupal system in just 6 months, demonstrating their capacity to handle large-scale, complex projects.

[Learn More](/companies/mochiya)

* * *

### Studio Umi

Studio Umi is a Japanese agency specializing in design, development, and operation of Drupal-based systems. They offer a complete range of services including planning, web marketing, infrastructure, UI design, development, and operations. Their team consists of certified experts focused on delivering complex and scalable web solutions with an Agile and flexible approach.

[Learn More](/companies/studio-umi)

* * *

### Texpress

Founded in 1999 and based in Tokyo, Texpress is a company specializing in system development and technological solutions. They position themselves as an acceleration partner for Digital Transformation, providing optimal solutions at maximum speed. Texpress offers expertise in Drupal CMS construction, custom development, and mobile/wearable application development.

[Learn More](/companies/texpress)
