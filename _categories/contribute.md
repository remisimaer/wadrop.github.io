This site is open source. You're welcome to contribute!

* * *

## How to Contribute

We welcome contributions from the community to help make WaDrop the most comprehensive resource for Drupal in Japan.

### For Developers

Open a pull request with your contribution on our GitHub repository:

[View on GitHub](https://github.com/remisimaer/wadrop.github.io)

### For Non-Technical Contributors

If you're not tech-savvy, you can simply open an issue and submit your contribution. We'll handle the technical implementation for you:

[Open an Issue](https://github.com/remisimaer/wadrop.github.io/issues)

### Join the Discussion

Have questions or ideas? Join the conversation on our GitHub Discussions:

[Join Discussions](https://github.com/remisimaer/wadrop.github.io/discussions)

* * *

## What You Can Contribute

- Add or update company profiles
- Submit new Drupal modules specific to Japan
- Share community resources
- Improve guides and documentation
- Report broken links or outdated information
- Translate content
- Suggest new categories or features

**Important:** If you contribute audiovisual content (images, videos, audio files), you must own the rights to that content or have explicit permission to use it. By submitting content, you confirm that you have the necessary rights and permissions.
