## General Information

ANNAI is a Tokyo-based company founded in 2007, specializing in web system development with Drupal. They focus on comprehensive support from development to maintenance, with strong technical expertise and an Agile approach. Their motto is: "ANNAI believes in 'total support from development to maintenance' to provide customers with the best service."

## Drupal Services

ANNAI offers:

- **System Development**: Design, development, and server management optimized for Drupal, using an Agile methodology that enables rapid adaptation to technological changes and client needs.
- **Consulting and Auditing**: Consulting and auditing services to evaluate existing systems, identify issues, and propose improvements.
- **Maintenance and Management**: Maintenance contracts to ensure continuous customization, security, and efficient system management.
- **Integration with Other Systems**: Experience in Drupal collaboration with other platforms, extending functionality and improving organizational efficiency.
- **Agile Development**: Iterative and flexible approach to respond quickly to changes and evolving needs.

## Target Sectors

ANNAI works with:

- **Regional Governments**: Development of sites and systems for local administrations.
- **Universities**: Creation of portals and systems for educational institutions.
- **Large Enterprises**: Collaboration with major companies for complex web solutions.

## Project Examples

- **METI's jGrants**: Web service improving grant application management, developed in Agile to adapt to environmental changes.
- **Group Module**: Contribution to the development of the Group module, enabling configuration of complex access controls in Drupal.

## Positioning

ANNAI positions itself as a technical expert and strategic partner, offering comprehensive support and recognized expertise in Drupal system development and maintenance. Their team is certified and experienced, capable of meeting complex client needs.

## Certifications and Partnerships

- Drupal 8 Grand Master and Drupal Technical Ambassador certifications.
- Partnerships with major Drupal ecosystem players.
