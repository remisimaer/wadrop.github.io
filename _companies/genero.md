## General Information

Genero Inc. is a Tokyo-based company specializing in digital transformation (DX) and Drupal-based system development. They focus on providing strategic, creative, and technical services to solve business challenges. Their team consists of bilingual experts (English and Japanese) and they offer comprehensive support from consulting to cloud operations. Their tagline is: "Genero Inc. is a one-stop system development company providing DX strategy, marketing, system development, and cloud operation services."

## Drupal Services

Genero Inc. offers:

- **Development and Maintenance**: Multilingual web and e-commerce solutions based on Drupal 9, with post-purchase support and continuous maintenance.
- **Consulting and Training**: Strategic and technical support to help clients get the most out of Drupal.
- **Integration and Customization**: Adapting Drupal to specific needs, integration with other systems, and custom feature development.
- **Multilingual Support**: Japanese-language assistance to facilitate local and international adoption.
- **Proprietary Product**: "Kamihaya," a platform for quickly building DX teams to accelerate digital transformation.

## Target Sectors

Genero Inc. operates in:

- **Japanese Enterprises**: Supporting digital transformation, website development, and portals.
- **E-commerce**: E-commerce site development with Drupal Commerce and product data management via the Contentserv platform (PIM).

## Project Examples

- **E-commerce Projects**: Development and migration of e-commerce sites to Drupal, improving operational efficiency and reducing costs.
- **Digital Transformation Projects**: Supporting the establishment of DX teams and developing advanced digital solutions.

## Positioning

Genero Inc. positions itself as a strategic partner and technical expert, offering comprehensive support for enterprise digital transformation via Drupal. Their approach focuses on innovation, flexibility, and rapid implementation.

## Certifications and Partnerships

- ISO/IEC 27001 and JIS Q 27001 certifications.
- Acquia Certified Site Builder and Developer certifications.
- Partnerships with Acquia and the Drupal Association, with strong community involvement (organizing meetups, sponsoring events).

## Online Presence

Genero Inc. has a blog, technical resources, and is active on LinkedIn, GitHub, and Drupal.org. They regularly organize events to promote open source and digital transformation.
