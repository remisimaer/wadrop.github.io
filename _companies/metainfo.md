## General Information

MetaInfo was founded in 2018 and is based in Tokyo, specializing in creating sustainable and scalable systems with Drupal. They emphasize the use of simple, combinable, and extensible functional blocks to meet client needs.

## Drupal Services

MetaInfo offers:

- **System Development**: Design and management of complex and scalable websites, using modular functional blocks.
- **Consulting and Auditing**: Evaluating Drupal's suitability for client needs, with tailored recommendations.
- **Maintenance and Management**: Maintenance contracts to ensure continuous customization and efficient management.
- **Integration with Other Systems**: Experience in Drupal collaboration with other platforms, extending functionality.
- **Agile and No-code/Low-code Development**: Flexible approach allowing users to modify and extend systems after initial construction.

## Target Sectors

MetaInfo operates in:

- **Education**: Development of academic archive portals, for example, for the University of Tokyo.
- **Large Enterprises**: Scalable and secure web solutions for major companies.
- **E-commerce**: E-commerce sites with complete product and order management.

## Project Examples

- **University of Tokyo Academic Archives Portal**: Academic archive portal enabling search and access to valuable collections.
- **Digital Archive System Meta Archive**: Platform for storing and publishing digitized cultural assets, reducing access costs to cultural resources.

## Positioning

MetaInfo positions itself as a technical expert and strategic partner, focused on creating scalable and sustainable systems. Their modular approach and strong involvement in the open-source community strengthen their credibility.

## Certifications and Partnerships

- ISO/IEC 27001 and JIS Q 27001 certifications.
- Acquia Certified Site Builder and Developer certifications.
- Partnerships with Acquia and the Drupal Association, with strong community involvement.
