## General Information

Mochiya is a Tokyo-based company specializing in the design, development, and operation of Drupal-based systems. They offer a comprehensive range of services, including planning, web marketing, infrastructure, UI design, design, development, and operations. Their team consists of certified experts and they focus on providing complex and scalable web solutions.

## Drupal Services

Mochiya offers:

- **System Development**: Design and management of complex websites, using modular and extensible functional blocks.
- **Consulting and Auditing**: Evaluating client needs and providing tailored recommendations to avoid compatibility issues and performance problems.
- **Maintenance and Management**: Maintenance contracts to ensure continuous customization and efficient management.
- **Integration with Other Systems**: Experience in Drupal collaboration with other platforms, extending functionality.
- **Agile and No-code/Low-code Development**: Flexible approach allowing users to modify and extend systems after initial construction.

## Target Sectors

Mochiya operates in:

- **Education**: Development of academic archive portals.
- **Large Enterprises**: Scalable and secure web solutions for major companies, including large-scale projects such as consolidating 1,500 internal NEC sites.
- **E-commerce**: E-commerce sites with complete product and order management.

## Project Examples

- **NEC Internal Site Consolidation**: Unified 1,500 internal NEC sites into a single Drupal system, completed in 6 months, demonstrating the ability to manage complex and large-scale projects.
- **Comprehensive Web Services**: Development, maintenance, server management, and complete website construction services.
