## General Information

Studio Umi is a Japanese company specializing in the design, development, and operation of Drupal-based systems. They offer a comprehensive range of services, including planning, web marketing, infrastructure, UI design, design, development, and operations. Their team consists of certified experts and they focus on providing complex and scalable web solutions.

## Drupal Services

Studio Umi offers:

- **System Development**: Design and management of complex websites, using modular and extensible functional blocks.
- **Consulting and Auditing**: Evaluating client needs and providing tailored recommendations to avoid compatibility issues and performance problems.
- **Maintenance and Management**: Maintenance contracts to ensure continuous customization and efficient management.
- **Integration with Other Systems**: Experience in Drupal collaboration with other platforms, extending functionality.
- **Agile and No-code/Low-code Development**: Flexible approach allowing users to modify and extend systems after initial construction.

## Target Sectors

Studio Umi operates in:

- **Education**: Development of academic archive portals.
- **Large Enterprises**: Scalable and secure web solutions for major companies.
- **E-commerce**: E-commerce sites with complete product and order management.

## Project Examples

- **E-commerce Projects**: Development and migration of e-commerce sites to Drupal, improving operational efficiency and reducing costs.
- **Digital Transformation Projects**: Supporting the establishment of DX teams and developing advanced digital solutions.

## Positioning

Studio Umi positions itself as a technical expert and strategic partner, offering comprehensive support for enterprise digital transformation via Drupal. Their approach focuses on innovation, flexibility, and rapid implementation.

## Certifications and Partnerships

- ISO/IEC 27001 and JIS Q 27001 certifications.
- Acquia Certified Site Builder and Developer certifications.
- Partnerships with Acquia and the Drupal Association, with strong community involvement.
