## General Information

Texpress is a Japanese company specializing in system development and technological solutions, positioned as an acceleration partner for the Digital Transformation (DX) of its clients. Founded on October 13, 1999, the company has over 25 years of experience operating from the heart of Tokyo. Their philosophy is to provide the "optimal solution" at maximum speed.

## Drupal Services

Texpress offers three main service areas, with a clear focus on Drupal expertise:

### Platform & CMS Construction

- **CMS Site Construction**: Building and operating robust websites and enterprise portals using Drupal
- **Content Management Design**: Conception of content management functions tailored to client needs
- **Operation Support**: Maintenance and ongoing support for websites

### Custom Development

- **Business System Development**: Design and development of custom business systems adapted to unique client requirements
- **API Construction**: Building programming interfaces for connection to external systems
- **Backend Web Development**: Custom software development meeting specific enterprise requirements

### Mobile & Wearable Development

- **Smartphone Applications**: Development for Android and iOS platforms
- **Wearable Devices**: Applications for SmartWatch and other connected wearable devices

## Target Sectors

Texpress works with:

- **Enterprise Clients**: Companies seeking digital transformation and robust web presence
- **Corporate Portals**: Organizations requiring content management and portal solutions
- **Multi-platform Projects**: Businesses needing integrated web, mobile, and API solutions
